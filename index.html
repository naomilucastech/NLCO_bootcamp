ABOUT THE EMPLOYMENT BOOTCAMP

"Hi. I’m Naomi Lucas and I led the design process for The Employment Bootcamp.

I’m not sure how you got here but I’m very glad you did. You’re reading this right now because you want more - More out of life. More for yourself. This is where we come in.

The Employment Bootcamp is designed as an adventure; a learning game delivered through short, interactive content and challenges that you must complete to progress to the end.

You embark on a transformational journey through various levels, receiving guides and completing quests to earn points and badges that showcase your skills and achievements. The version of you that shows up on the other side will be your very best self - gainfully employed, with very high job prospects and a nimble toolkit to help navigate your career. And the future I just described..."

STILL HAVE QUESTIONS?

You are eligible if you:
  • Graduated from any tertiary institution (Technical College, University, Mono/Polytechnic, Vocational School etc.)
  • Have between 1 and 8 years work experience (That means you graduated no earlier than 2015 and have interned and/or worked with one or more organisations)
  • Are unemployed, under-employed or self-employed and need decent work
  • Can speak and write in English clearly, as the program will be conducted in English
  • Have a functional laptop or smartphone (As the bootcamp is delivered entirely online)
  • Can afford data to guarantee your uninterrupted participation in the bootcamp
  • Can commit at least 1 hour 30 minutes daily to participate in the bootcamp
  • Know how to use basic productivity tools (Like Google workspace, Slack etc.) and having a track record of leadership are added advantages
