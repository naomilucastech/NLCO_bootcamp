ABOUT THE EMPLOYMENT BOOTCAMP

"Hi. I’m Naomi Lucas and I led the design process for The Employment Bootcamp.

I’m not sure how you got here but I’m very glad you did. You’re reading this right now because you want more - More out of life. More for yourself. This is where we come in.

The Employment Bootcamp is designed as an adventure delivered through short, interactive content and challenges that you must complete to progress to the end.

You embark on a transformational journey through various levels, receiving guides and completing quests to earn badges that showcase your skills and achievements. The version of you that shows up on the other side will be your very best self - gainfully employed, with very high job prospects and a nimble toolkit to help you get and stay ahead. And the future I just described starts with your application"

TO PARTICIPATE IN THE BOOTCAMP...

You are expected to:

Be able to speak and write in English (as the program will be conducted in English)

Have a functional laptop or smartphone ( The bootcamp is delivered entirely online )

Commit at least 1 hour 30 minutes daily to participate in the bootcamp

Be able to join weekly live events ( West African Time )

Pay a commitment fee ( Check the FAQ section for details of the fee and funding options )

STILL HAVE QUESTIONS?

You are eligible if you:
  • Graduated from any post-secondary institution (Technical College, University, Mono/Polytechnic, Vocational School etc.) anywhere in the world.
  • Have between 1 and 8 years work experience (Internship experience also counts)
  • Are unemployed or self-employed and actively searching for work
  • Own or have access to an internet enabled laptop/smart device to guarantee your uninterrupted participation in the bootcamp

Click on any of the buttons on this website.

We take applications on a rolling basis. You can always apply. If you miss a cohort, you are automatically waitlisted. We reach out to you once we commence admissions for the next cohort.

The Employment Bootcamp was designed for those who have completed post-secondary education and have some work experience. If you are still in school or do not have any work experience, you are not eligible to apply at this time. You can always apply once you become eligible

Yes. If you are selected, you will be required to pay a commitment fee.

For Applicants based in Nigeria: Once selected, you will be required to pay a commitment fee of N55,000 (Fifty five thousand naira only)
For Applicants based abroad: (Any country outside of Nigeria): Once selected, you will be required to pay a commitment fee of $100 (One hundred US dollars)

We understand that this may be a significant investment for some people, so we have created a range of funding options. We encourage you to apply. If you are selected you can apply for financial aid to cover up to 30% of your commitment fee. You may also choose to pay in instalments using our pay small small plan.

Your application may be rejected for eligibility reasons. You can always reapply if you still wish to be a part of the program.

The Employment Bootcamp is 100% online. You will need an internet-enabled device, such as a smartphone, tablet, laptop, or desktop computer to access the learning platform and to participate in weekly live sessions and related activities.

Even better! For every challenge you complete, you will get a shareable, verifiable digital badge. At the end of the bootcamp, you will get a certificate of completion as well. You will also get a private url where you can manage all the credentials you receive.

We are working with recruiters across the globe to help our graduates secure decent jobs. As a participant of the program, you have direct access to these recruiters. From time to time, we also post Internships, Fellowships, Volunteer and Full/Part-time opportunities, so join our Slack community and follow us on social media to be the first to know.

Upon completion of The Employment Bootcamp, there will be full-time, project-based and volunteer roles open to suitable participants of the program. You may apply for any that you feel qualified for.
